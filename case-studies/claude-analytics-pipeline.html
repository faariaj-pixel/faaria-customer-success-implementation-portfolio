Case study

Claude Monthly Sales Analytics Pipeline

A repeatable analytics workflow that turns monthly sales and inventory CSVs into dashboards and executive summaries.

Contact FaariaBack to portfolio

Challenge

Monthly reporting can become manual, error-prone, and inconsistent when data cleaning, merging, KPI calculation, and presentation are handled separately.

Approach

  • Standardized order IDs, product SKUs, and customer names.
  • Merged sales and inventory cost data with validation.
  • Generated KPI summaries by product, category, and customer.
  • Produced dashboard and executive PowerPoint outputs.

Outputs

  • Cleaned CSV files
  • Merged monthly data files
  • Summary CSVs
  • HTML dashboards
  • Executive summary PowerPoint decks

Relevance to Customer Success & Implementation

Relevant to implementation and customer operations because successful teams need consistent KPI definitions, repeatable reporting, and clear dashboards that turn operating data into decisions.
