Case study

AI Workflow Automation Engagement

A business-first automation engagement for mapping manual workflows and implementing practical AI-supported systems.

Contact FaariaBack to portfolio

Challenge

Service businesses often know AI could help, but their workflows are undocumented, repetitive, and difficult to prioritize for automation.

Approach

  • Assessed repetitive workflows, bottlenecks, handoffs, data readiness, and business impact.
  • Aligned automation opportunities to effort, risk, readiness, and measurable outcomes.
  • Defined the 4A AI Efficiency Method: Assess, Align, Automate, Adopt.

Outputs

  • AI Workflow Readiness Map
  • Automation roadmap
  • SOP/user guide and team adoption plan
  • Discovery-call and offer positioning

Relevance to Customer Success & Implementation

This mirrors implementation work because it starts with workflow clarity, risk, process fit, documentation, and adoption before adding AI tools.
